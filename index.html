About Me

I am an 19-year-old engineering student with a confident passion for writing. Mostly, all the works I have done are by myself, for myself and not for any agency. I write what I feel, and that makes me feel good about writing, it knows no bounds and it never will. Although, I did a couple of product documentation to explore other niches of writing. I do photography as my hobby and express every picture with a brief description of it. I am also a bibliophile, and the love for new novels and books increase day by day for me. I am right now working on a story which will hopefully release in the second half of next year. Do head to the blog section to find more articles of mine! Happy reading. Feel free to contact me if you want any mental support or anything for that matter.
